# Reviewer Attacks

- The evidence is synthetic and may not transfer to real hardware.
- The prior proposed composer is already strong, so v5 must show that barrier certification adds something beyond tuning.
- Risk gating may hide hard cases through abstention.
- Energy-basin diagnostics may not correspond to learned robot controllers without released checkpoints.
- The literature boundary may be too close to composable energy policies, DMP skill chaining, options, and task-and-motion planning.

Response: the paper emphasizes seam-specific claims, fixed-risk coverage and breach, ablations, explicit failure conditions, and honest local scope. It should not be submitted as ICLR-main ready until external validation, hardware/high-fidelity logs, checkpoints, videos, independent baselines, and full manual related work are available.
